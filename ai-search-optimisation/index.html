02 — What Is AEO

What Is AI Search Optimisation (AEO)?

Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO) is the practice of structuring your online presence so AI models can find, understand, and cite you. Our Answer Engine Optimisation service covers all three layers.

The difference between SEO and AEO

Traditional SEO gets you a ranking in a list of blue links. AEO gets you cited as the answer. When a buyer asks ChatGPT "who should I contact for HDB resale in Tampines?" — you want to be the agent it names. That's a fundamentally different goal requiring a different strategy, built on authoritative content and structured signals.

How AI models select which agents to mention

AI models are trained to cite sources they consider authoritative, consistent, and credible. For property agents, this means: a well-structured website with clear specialisation signals, positive and consistent reviews across platforms, citations from credible property-related sources, and schema markup that tells AI models exactly who you are and what you do.

Why your website's content structure determines your AI visibility

AI models read your site like a very fast, very literal researcher. If your site doesn't clearly state your estate, your specialisation, your transaction history, and your service area — it will not cite you. Content structure is the foundation of AEO, and it's where most property agent websites fall short within the first 30 seconds of analysis.

03 — GEO Explained

What Is GEO — Generative Engine Optimisation?

GEO goes beyond individual answers

While AEO focuses on getting you cited in direct question-and-answer AI responses, GEO — Generative Engine Optimisation — focuses on building the broader information environment that shapes how AI models understand your positioning in the market. Our GEO strategy for property agents builds the signals that make AI models consistently associate your name with your estate and specialisation.

Why GEO matters as AI search matures

As AI search platforms grow more sophisticated, they begin to develop persistent understanding of who the authoritative voices are in any given field. GEO is the practice of systematically building that reputation across every platform AI models index — so that your name becomes associated with Singapore property expertise at the model level, not just in individual search results. This is where the competitive advantage compounds over time.
