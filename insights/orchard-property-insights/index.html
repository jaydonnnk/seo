Orchard Property Insights — Market Data, Search Trends, and Agent Landscape 2026

Overview

Orchard at a Glance — Key Market Facts

Almost entirely private condo and luxury residential — no HDB flats, minimal EC presence. This is Singapore's premium private residential corridor, with estimated 15,000+ private condo and apartment units across the core Orchard and River Valley corridor.

Districts covered: D09, D10, and city fringe overlap — core Orchard (D09 Orchard/River Valley), Bukit Timah/Holland corridor (D10), with overlap into D01/D02 for city fringe developments marketed to a similar buyer profile.

Foreign buyer presence and ABSD impact — the 60% ABSD rate for foreign buyers (introduced April 2023) materially reduced individual foreign purchases. Corporate and fund buyers, permanent residents, and high-net-worth nationals from ABSD-exempt treaty countries remain active. Lower volume, significantly higher per-transaction value.

Lower transaction volume, significantly higher transaction value — one Orchard or Nassim transaction can generate more commission than 5–6 HDB deals. Agent SEO ROI per keyword ranking is correspondingly higher.

Unit counts are estimates. Verify current figures via URA REALIS, data.gov.sg, or URA's private property data releases.

Foreign and Expat Demand

Foreign Buyer and Expat Demand in Orchard 2026

How ABSD Has Reshaped Foreign Buyer Demand

The April 2023 increase of Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty to 60% for foreign nationals buying Singapore residential property materially reduced individual overseas buyer transactions in Orchard. The profile has shifted: corporate entities and funds (which may have different ABSD treatment), permanent residents (who pay 5% ABSD on a first property), and buyers from ABSD-exempt treaty countries remain active. Agents advising foreign buyers must be proficient in ABSD calculations and structuring options — a knowledge gap that represents a clear differentiation opportunity for specialists who publish ABSD guides. For current ABSD rates, refer to IRAS.

Which Nationalities Are Buying in Orchard?

The high-net-worth buyer profile in Orchard has historically included mainland Chinese buyers (the largest non-Singapore nationality group pre-ABSD), Indonesian HNW buyers (long-term Singapore property investors), Malaysian buyers (often PRs or those structuring around ABSD), and Western European buyers (finance and tech executives on Singapore assignment). Post-2023 ABSD, the profile has rebalanced toward PRs, Singapore Citizens making first or second private purchases, and the smaller cohort of ABSD-exempt treaty nationals. Search behaviour differs by nationality — Chinese buyers tend to research via Chinese-language platforms and WeChat; Indonesian buyers rely on referral networks; Western buyers use Google and LinkedIn searches before relocating.

Competition

The Orchard Property Agent Landscape

How Many Agents Specialise in Orchard?

Many generalist agents claim Orchard or D09 expertise, but few have verifiable track records of D09/D10 transactions at the luxury tier. The CEA public register at cea.gov.sg is the authoritative source for agent registration and any publicly available transaction data. Buyers at the luxury tier increasingly want verifiable transaction history before engaging — agents who surface this credibly online have a decisive advantage.

What Luxury Buyers Look for in an Agent

Verifiable D09/D10 Transaction Track Record

Luxury buyers research agents as thoroughly as they research the property. Agents without publicly visible transaction history are effectively invisible to this buyer type.

Language and Cultural Competence

Mandarin capability for mainland Chinese and Malaysian Chinese buyers; Bahasa Indonesia for Indonesian buyers; English fluency for Western expat and international buyers. Agents who signal multilingual ability in their online presence widen their buyer pool.

ABSD Calculation Expertise

Foreign buyers and PRs need accurate ABSD guidance before committing. Agents who can run through ABSD scenarios competently — including structuring options — differentiate clearly from generalists.

The Trust Deficit in Orchard Agent Marketing

Most Orchard agents rely on developer referrals, agency network introductions, and word-of-mouth. Very few appear on Google when international buyers search before relocating — terms like "property agent Orchard Singapore" or "buy luxury condo Singapore foreigner" return portals and generic directory listings rather than specialist agent websites. The HNW buyer who arrives in Singapore with a shortlist will have compiled that list before landing — agents who are not visible online are not on it.
