Search Trend Analysis

Google Keyword Planner and Search Console data to track query volume shifts, seasonal patterns, and emerging search behaviours across Singapore property categories.

Keyword Volume Research

Estate-level search volumes and competition scores — from broad terms like "property agent Singapore" down to specific queries like "sell HDB Tampines" or "buy condo Jurong."

Tracking how ChatGPT and Perplexity respond to Singapore property queries — which agents are cited, what information surfaces, and where the gaps are.
