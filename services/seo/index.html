Property SEO fixes that. We identify the exact searches buyers and sellers in your area are making, then structure your site to answer those searches better than anyone else.

FAQs

SEO questions answered plainly.

The most valuable keywords are specific: "property agent Tampines HDB", "sell 4 room flat Tampines", "buy EC Punggol". Broad terms like "property agent Singapore" are dominated by large portals. Specific, location-intent keywords are where independent agents can win — and where buyers are closest to making contact.
Property search in Singapore has its own logic — town-based, flat-type based, and transaction-intent based. Buyers search by MRT, by launch name, by scheme (BTO, EC, resale). A generic SEO approach misses all of that. Property SEO is built around how Singapore buyers actually search, not generic keyword theory.
Individual agents can absolutely rank — and often have an advantage over large agency sites on specific local searches because they can go deeper on one area. A dedicated page about selling HDB flats in Tampines, written from genuine expertise, will rank better than a generic agency page that tries to cover everywhere.
First rankings usually appear within 60–90 days. Inbound leads start around month 3–4. The compounding effect — more keywords, more traffic, consistent inbound — builds from month 6. This isn't a 2-week fix. It's a system that earns compound returns over time.
Yes. On-page covers your site structure, content, technical health, and schema markup. Off-page covers backlinks, branded mentions, PR placements, and authority signals. Both are required to rank — and both compound over time when built correctly.
