Why one platform isn't enough

Your buyers are distributed. Your visibility needs to be too.

A buyer in their 30s might start on Google. Their spouse might ask ChatGPT. Their parent might search Bing. Their colleague might check Perplexity. When they arrange a viewing, they'll check Google Maps.

Each of these platforms pulls information from different sources. Being visible on one doesn't transfer to the others. An agent who optimises only for Google is invisible to a growing share of property buyers.

Multi-platform distribution means we build your presence intentionally across every channel your buyers use — so whichever one they start from, you're the name they find.

Google AI Overviews pulls from different sources than ChatGPT

Google prioritises structured content and established sites. ChatGPT weights training data and browsed content. Each needs its own strategy.

ChatGPT pulls from different sources than Perplexity

Perplexity relies heavily on live web search and citations. ChatGPT draws more from training data and broader authority signals. Both require dedicated optimisation.

We cover all platforms simultaneously

We build one integrated system — not separate campaigns. The same content and authority signals serve multiple platforms at once wherever possible, maximising efficiency.
