FAQs

AEO questions answered plainly.

AEO is the practice of structuring your content so that AI-powered answer systems — like Google AI Overviews — select and display your content when users ask questions. Instead of ranking as a link, your answer appears directly in the search result. For property agents, it means your name and expertise appear when buyers ask property questions — before they even see traditional listings.
SEO focuses on ranking as a clickable link in search results. AEO focuses on being the source Google's AI uses to generate direct answers — the content that appears above the links. SEO and AEO are complementary: a strong SEO foundation makes AEO easier, but AEO requires additional content structuring, schema markup, and question-answer formatting that standard SEO doesn't cover.
Yes — and it happens more than most agents realise. Google AI Overviews appear on informational queries like "how do I sell my HDB flat in Singapore" or "what should I look for in a property agent in Tampines". Agents who have well-structured, authoritative content on these topics get cited. Individual agents can compete here because depth of local expertise beats breadth.
We identify questions buyers ask in your area, structure content to answer them directly and concisely, implement FAQ and HowTo schema markup, create dedicated Q&A pages, and ensure your content establishes clear entity associations (you as a property agent in a specific location). The goal is for Google's AI to identify your content as the most reliable answer.
First AI Overview appearances typically come within 60–90 days of content going live, assuming the underlying SEO foundation is solid. Some queries are less competitive and show faster results. Others take longer because the AI requires multiple authoritative sources corroborating the same information. We track appearances and expand coverage monthly.
