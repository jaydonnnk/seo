The opportunity

Buyers search by area. You need to rank in their area.

No buyer types 'property agent Singapore'. They type the exact estate they're looking in — and they're ready to transact when they do.

01 Specific beats broad

'Property agent Tampines HDB' has less competition and higher buying intent than 'property agent Singapore'. Local keywords convert at a higher rate.

02 Google rewards local expertise

A page dedicated to selling HDB flats in Tampines outranks a generic page from PropNex or ERA — because Google's local algorithm favours specificity.

03 AI searches are local too

When buyers ask ChatGPT 'who is the best property agent in Tampines?', the answer pulls from local authority signals — not generic brand size.

Regular SEO focuses on ranking for broad topic-based searches. Local SEO focuses on ranking for searches that include a specific location — and appearing in Google's local pack (the map results). For property agents, local SEO also involves Google Business Profile optimisation, local schema markup, area-specific content pages, and local citation building. Both are part of what we do — local SEO is the layer that drives the most qualified traffic.
