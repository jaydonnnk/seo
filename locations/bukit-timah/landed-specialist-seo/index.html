Stage 2 Research — "What should I know about GCBs / semi-Ds?"

Buyer Context

Understanding the Landed Buyer in Singapore

The landed segment has regulatory and financial complexity that shapes how buyers search. Great SEO content addresses these complexities directly.

PR & Approval Requirements

Singapore PRs require SLA approval to purchase landed property. Content that explains this process clearly attracts high-intent buyers who are deep in the decision process.

GCB Area Restrictions

GCB Areas carry specific URA planning restrictions on plot size, development, and use. Buyers search for agents who understand these rules — not just ones who can open doors.

School Ballot Strategy

Families buying school-belt landed need to understand the P1 registration phases and 1km radius requirements. Address this in your content and you become their trusted advisor before any transaction.
