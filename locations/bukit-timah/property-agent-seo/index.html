The Opportunity

Why Bukit Timah Agents Need a Different SEO Strategy

The landed segment is unlike any other. Buyers are fewer, transactions are higher-value, and the due diligence period is longer. Your SEO must reflect that journey.

Low Transaction Volume, High Intent

GCB and landed transactions are rare. Each search is a serious buyer. Volume-based SEO tactics don't apply — you need intent-led, authority-driven content.

Multiple Distinct Buyer Profiles

UHNW GCB buyers, school-belt semi-D families, expat condo seekers, and en-bloc upgrade buyers all search differently. Generic content misses each one.

Long Research Cycles

A GCB buyer may research for 6–18 months before transacting. You need content that captures them early and keeps you top-of-mind through a long consideration phase.
